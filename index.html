The World of Heroica

Heroica is a verdant world with high magic potential and naturally occuring portals to other worlds through points of great natural beauty and power (waterfalls, mountains, ocean depths, caverns. Portals can also occur close to extremely powerful animals, monsters, spirits and gods. Portals The portals to the near worlds are open - which means lots of people know about them and anyone can use them to travel. There are also secret portals that require a special key to use.

Game Rules

Game Rules Going for a mix between Cypher System and Nights Black Agents here. Grade and Experience Your grade is your level in Academie Heroica and shows how much Effort you can use to make a challenge easier. It starts at 1. You improve your grade through experience. You’ll normally get between 2 and 4 experience for each session you play depending on how much fun you have, how much cool stuff you do and how much you learn.
