Teachers

The faculty of the Academie were all heroes in their own right from across worlds both near and far. They now dedicate themselves to training the heroes of the future. They are highly capable and… unique individuals.

Most teachers will be Level 5-6, but might be level 7 or 8 in their areas of specialty.

Merlin -

An ancient, slightly mad and powerful wizard and the principal of Lycee Heroica

Wolf Sensei ->

A tall, scarred wolf-man wearing a kimono who teaches close combat and is a master of kenjutsu

Minako ->

A small, pretty woman with large brown eyes. Minako is a ninja and teaches stealth, evasion and ninjitsu

Professer Webb ->

A tall, lanky, wild-haired scientist who teaches experimentation and how to build gadgetry and gizmos that help heroes save the day

Doctor Wipple -

Scientist and chemistry teacher, potions and genetics
